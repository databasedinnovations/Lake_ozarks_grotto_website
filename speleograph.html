About The Speleograph

The Speleograph is Lake Ozarks Grotto's official publication, sharing stories from our underground adventures, documenting important conservation work, and keeping our community connected. Each issue features expedition reports, technical articles, cave science, safety tips, and news from the Missouri caving community.

Contribute to The Speleograph

The Speleograph thrives on contributions from our members! We welcome submissions that share your caving experiences, technical knowledge, and passion for Missouri's caves. Whether you're a seasoned caver or new to the grotto, your perspective matters.

What We're Looking For:

  • Trip reports from recent expeditions
  • Technical articles on caving techniques
  • Cave photography and artwork
  • Conservation project updates
  • Historical stories and grotto memories
  • Equipment reviews and recommendations
  • Cave science and geology articles
  • Safety tips and lessons learned
